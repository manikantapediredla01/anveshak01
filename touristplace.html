Geofencing & Border Safety Information

What is Geofencing?

Geofencing creates virtual boundaries around tourist areas. When you enter or exit these zones, our system automatically monitors your safety and can alert authorities if needed.

  • Real-time location monitoring within safe zones
  • Automatic alerts if you deviate from safe areas
  • Emergency response coordination
